Books and Curriculum

We follow NCF for foundation stages. NCF stands for National Curriculum Framework. It's a document that outlines the goals, principles, and guidelines for developing and implementing school curriculum in India. The NCF is based on principles that emphasize the importance of holistic development, learner-centeredness, and the development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
